Homemade Explosives (HME) and Precursor Chemicals Awareness for Public Safety Course

The Homemade Explosives (HME) and Precursor Chemicals Awareness for Public Safety Course consists of five modules.These modules are designed to help raise or maintain awareness of signs of HME, precursor chemicals, and other bomb-making materials while conducting public safety activities.

The five modules can be found in the link below with sections titled:

  • Module 1: HME: An Imminent Danger
  • Module 2: Who Makes Homemade Explosives?
  • Module 3: What Should I Look For?
  • Module 4: What To Do If You Suspect The Presence Of HME?
  • Module 5: What Would You Do If...?

Estimated time of completion: 75 minutes
